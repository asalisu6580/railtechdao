About Us

We use DAO principles (transparent decisions, working groups, contributor rewards, rotation of roles) while staying lean and compliant as a student body.

RailTech DAO is a student-led, innovation-driven community focused on advancing railway engineering education and technology in Nigeria starting in Kaduna State and scaling nationwide. We blend education, hands-on projects, and sustainable funding so students learn, build, and earn while bridging the gap between classroom theory and industry practice.

Read More

Our Mission

Empower railway engineering students with skills, tools, and opportunities to become industry-ready innovators, while uniting students, lecturers, alumni, and professionals around tangible projects and events.

Our Vision

Become Africa’s Leading Student-Driven Railway Innovation Hub, Where Students Learn, Build, And Earn While Delivering Solutions That Transform Railway Education And Practice.
